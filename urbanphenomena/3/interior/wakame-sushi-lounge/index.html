Wakame Sushi Lounge

Location:
Al-Zahra District
Area:
175 M2
Year:
2013

Intuitive in expression, Wakame has become the prime destination for Sushi lovers in only two years, especially after Sushi gained a lot of recognition in becoming a staple dinning out cuisine for locals.

The main inspiration came from nature, fishing villages and rice paddies of Japan. Wakame (seaweed in Japanese) wants to take customers through a journey that appeal to the senses with a balanced dose of Japan, a juxtaposition of the Japanese dining experience against locale taste and sensibilities. The main materials used are Bamboo, Stone, Birch, Linen and Rattan all of whom are organic to harmonize the overall experience. The design invokes an international environment with a tone of cultured modernity, flexibility, ample use of quality natural materials, excellent lighting, clarity and functionality.
